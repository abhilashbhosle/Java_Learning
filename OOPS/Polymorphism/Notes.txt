Poly means many morphism means behaviour.
It is divided into two types 1.RunTime Polymorphism 2.CompileTime Polymorphism. 

1.RunTime Polymorphism.
=======================
The execution or behaviour is known during runtime is called runtime polymorphism 
eg. Method Overloading.

1.CompileTime Polymorphism.
=======================
The execution or behaviour is known during compilation is called compileTime polymorphism 
eg. Method Overriding.
